Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

InvoTrack is designed with privacy first. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or business information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no data.

InvoTrack operates entirely offline. All information you enter into the app—including jobs, clients, invoices, time logs, and settings—is stored locally on your device only.

What this means:

  • No account registration required
  • No cloud storage or sync
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data sharing with third parties

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage provided by your operating system. This includes:

  • Job information and status
  • Client names and contact details
  • Invoice data and history
  • Time tracking logs
  • Company profiles and payment details
  • App settings and preferences

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it.

3. Data Export & Backup

InvoTrack provides features to export your data:

  • CSV Export: Export jobs, clients, and invoices to CSV format
  • Backup: Create a full backup file of all app data
  • PDF Generation: Generate invoice PDFs for sharing

When you use these features, the files are saved to your device or shared through your device's share functionality. We have no access to these files.

4. Permissions

InvoTrack may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage: To save exported files, backups, and your company logo
  • Notifications: To show timer alerts when tracking time on jobs

All permissions are optional and the app functions without them (with limited features).

5. Third-Party Services

InvoTrack does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app is completely self-contained.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your device's built-in security (PIN, fingerprint, face ID) protects your data
  • Data is stored in the app's private storage area, inaccessible to other apps
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data

We recommend using the backup feature regularly to prevent data loss.

7. Children's Privacy

InvoTrack is a business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Since we don't collect any data, significant changes are unlikely.
