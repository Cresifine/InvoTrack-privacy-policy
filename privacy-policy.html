InvoTrack - Job & Invoice Management

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Overview

InvoTrack ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: InvoTrack is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

The app allows you to store the following information locally on your device:

  • Business information (company name, address, contact details)
  • Client information (names, addresses, contact details)
  • Job details (descriptions, pricing, time logs)
  • Invoice data (amounts, dates, payment status)
  • Service/worker rates and descriptions
  • Payment method information (bank details, PayPal, crypto wallets)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into InvoTrack is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive database). This means:

  • Your data stays on your device - We have no access to it
  • No cloud sync - Data is not uploaded to any servers
  • No account required - You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app
  • Export control - You have full control over exporting and backing up your data

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your device's built-in security features protect your data
  • We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID)
  • Regularly back up your data using the app's export feature
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data

Third-Party Services

InvoTrack does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app may use the following device capabilities:

  • Notifications - For timer alerts (optional, requires permission)
  • File System - For exporting PDFs and backups
  • Sharing - For sharing invoices and quotes via your device's share feature

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access - All your data is visible within the app
  • Export - Export your data anytime using the backup feature
  • Delete - Delete individual records or all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability - Export data in JSON format for use elsewhere

Children's Privacy

InvoTrack is a business tool intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information regardless of user age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
